What is ClarifyPath?

ClarifyPath is a guided 5-session process based on modern coaching psychology, career reinvention frameworks, and strategic decision-making tools.

How to start

The 5 ClarifyPath Sessions

  • SPARK – Energy, motivation, and meaning
  • SHAPE – Strengths, talents, natural patterns
  • SHIFT – What must evolve and why
  • SYSTEMS – Matching you with the right career “system”
  • SOAR – Identity + 90-day action plan

Who is ClarifyPath for?

For people who feel lost, stuck, multi-potential, curious, or unsure about their next step.
