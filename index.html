How the 5-session program works

The AI coach will guide you through five structured sessions. Each session builds on the last to lead you toward clarity and action.

  • SPARK – Energy, motivation, purpose.
  • SHAPE – Strengths, talents, natural patterns.
  • SHIFT – What must change and why.
  • SYSTEMS – Your ideal career ecosystem.
  • SOAR – Identity + 90-day action plan.

Who is this for?

ClarifyPath is designed for people who:

  • Are multi-potential and curious
  • Like many things and struggle to choose
  • Feel stuck or misaligned in their current path
  • Want meaningful, strategic or impactful work
  • Prefer deep, structured questions

You don’t need clarity to begin — this process creates clarity.

What you’ll walk away with

By the end of the 5 sessions, you will have:

  • A clear picture of your strengths, motivations, and patterns
  • 2–3 aligned career directions
  • A language to describe your professional identity
  • A realistic, actionable 90-day plan
  • A reusable method for making future career decisions
